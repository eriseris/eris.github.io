Developing a layout for a school organization entails creating a structured, visually appealing, and functional framework for displaying the organization's purpose, activities, members, and other pertinent information. This design can be used for a website, brochure, or internal communication platform.

Select a color palette that complements the school's colors or the organization's theme, ensuring visual consistency throughout the layout. Use fonts that are clear and readable, professional but friendly, and appropriate for both students and faculty.
