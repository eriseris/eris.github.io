My Experience

At first, putting up a price was hard to decide, because I was just starting back then, that is why I always let the customer decide for its price or how much their budget would be. Unless the customer insisted for me to give the price for my service. It put so much pressure for me while doing the work because what if the customer didn't like the outcome, I’m afraid of not meeting their expectations. But along the process, I tried consulting with the customer if I am visualising their expected outcome right. If the customer gets satisfied it overwhelms me a lot, since they deserve the right service in paying for it and what overwhelms me the most is that little by little I know that I can inspire someone to step up with their comfort zone by sharing my skills through paid and freelance commissions. In the long run, pressure is still there because all of the customers have different expectations and appreciations and by that, I've learned to grind and hustle more to learn new things, and help myself improve each day
