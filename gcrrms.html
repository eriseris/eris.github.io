Gordon College Raffle and Recitation Management System

By Joefrey on April 20, 2021 • 5 mins read
Scroll

About

Developing a raffle system for a school entails creating a fair, engaging, and transparent way to raise funds or distribute prizes at school events. This system can be used to increase participation and excitement at fundraisers, school fairs, and community events.

A random number generator can be used to select winners. This method ensures transparency and fairness, and the results can be broadcast live on a screen or via social media. Clearly define the rules, prizes, and conditions to ensure transparency and broad participation from students, parents, and staff.

The goal is to increase participation, offer exciting prizes, and make the raffle more accessible while supporting school programs and initiatives through a modern, user-friendly platform.

The college can use it when an event occurs or as a teaching tool to keep students engaged in class. Technology used included HTML, Javascript, Bootstrap, and PHP.
